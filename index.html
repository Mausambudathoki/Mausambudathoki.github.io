About

I am an interdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of sustainable aquaculture, aquatic food systems, environmental assessment, and food-system transitions.

My research examines how aquatic foods can contribute to sustainable diets, environmental resilience, circular food systems, and future food-system transformation across diverse socio-ecological contexts.

I combine life cycle assessment, systematic evidence synthesis, consumer research, machine learning, text mining, and mixed-method approaches to generate policy-relevant insights for sustainable blue food systems.

Current Focus

Sustainable aquaculture, environmental impacts of aquatic food production, food-system resilience, and sustainability transitions within blue food systems.

Research

My research integrates environmental, nutritional, socio-economic, and behavioural dimensions of aquatic foods to support more sustainable and resilient food systems.

Sustainable Aquaculture

Environmental performance, production systems, circularity, and sustainable aquatic food production.

Blue Food Systems

Aquatic foods, marine food futures, sustainable diets, and food-system resilience.

Life Cycle Assessment

LCA, environmental benchmarking, emissions assessment, and resource-efficiency analysis.

Consumer Behaviour

Aquatic food consumption, public perceptions, sustainability communication, and market dynamics.

Food-System Transitions

Sustainability transitions, circular systems, socio-ecological change, and responsible innovation.

Computational Methods

Machine learning, text mining, systematic reviews, meta-analysis, and data-driven food-system research.

Projects

OLAMUR

Offshore Low-Trophic Aquaculture in Multi-Use Platforms

Sustainability systems analysis and integrated environmental assessment of offshore aquaculture systems, including LCA, techno-economic analysis, environmental benchmarking, and circular production opportunities.

CLIDA

Climate Impact of Danish Aquaculture

Climate-related environmental assessment and sustainability benchmarking of Danish aquaculture systems to inform evidence-based sustainability planning.

SeaFree

Seaweed Synergy: Enabling Emission-Free Seafood

Systems-oriented sustainability assessment of seaweed-based food production, circular economy strategies, emissions reduction, and blue food innovation.

EATFISH

Innovative Training Network for Sustainable Aquaculture

Interdisciplinary research on sustainability transitions, environmental performance, consumer dynamics, and socio-economic dimensions of aquatic food systems.

FOODRUS

Circular Solutions for Food Waste Reduction

Research on circular food systems, sustainability transitions, resource efficiency, food waste reduction, and environmental performance.
