About

I am an interdisciplinary researcher focusing on sustainable aquaculture, aquatic food systems, environmental assessment, and sustainable food-system transitions. My research examines how aquatic foods can contribute to sustainable diets, environmental resilience, and future food systems across diverse socio-ecological contexts.

My work integrates quantitative and mixed-method approaches, including systematic reviews, evidence synthesis, life cycle assessment, consumer research, machine learning, text mining, and systems-oriented sustainability analysis.
