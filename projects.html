Projects

Selected research projects in sustainable aquaculture, blue food systems, circularity, and environmental assessment.

Projects

OLAMUR

Offshore Low-Trophic Aquaculture in Multi-Use Platforms

Sustainability systems analysis and integrated environmental assessment of offshore aquaculture systems, including LCA, techno-economic analysis, environmental benchmarking, and circular production opportunities.

CLIDA

Climate Impact of Danish Aquaculture

Climate-related environmental assessment and sustainability benchmarking of Danish aquaculture systems to inform evidence-based sustainability planning.

SeaFree

Seaweed Synergy: Enabling Emission-Free Seafood

Systems-oriented sustainability assessment of seaweed-based food production, circular economy strategies, emissions reduction, and blue food innovation.

EATFISH

Innovative Training Network for Sustainable Aquaculture

Interdisciplinary research on sustainability transitions, environmental performance, consumer dynamics, and socio-economic dimensions of aquatic food systems.

FOODRUS

Circular Solutions for Food Waste Reduction

Research on circular food systems, sustainability transitions, resource efficiency, food waste reduction, and environmental performance.
